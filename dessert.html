Desserts

Brownies

Rich, fudgy squares of decadent chocolate baked to perfection — crisp on the edges with a soft, gooey center. Made with premium cocoa and real butter, our brownies are a timeless treat, served warm and optionally topped with a scoop of vanilla ice cream and a drizzle of chocolate sauce.

Ice Cream

A classic frozen dessert made from rich cream and real ingredients, churned to smooth, creamy perfection. Choose from a variety of timeless and seasonal flavors, including velvety vanilla, decadent chocolate, and refreshing strawberry. Served by the scoop and perfect on its own or paired with your favorite dessert.

Rice Pudding

A warm and comforting classic made with slow-cooked rice, fresh milk, and a touch of sugar, gently simmered to creamy perfection. Lightly spiced with cinnamon and vanilla, and finished with a sprinkle of raisins or a dash of nutmeg for a timeless, homestyle dessert.

Molten Cake

A rich, individual chocolate cake with a soft, gooey center that flows like molten lava when cut open. Baked to perfection and served warm with a dusting of powdered sugar and a scoop of vanilla ice cream. A decadent dessert for true chocolate lovers.
