Pizza

Margherita pizza

Margherita pizza is a classic Italian pizza known for its simplicity and fresh ingredients.
It typically features a thin crust topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese

Chicken BBQ pizza

Chicken BBQ pizza is a popular and flavorful twist on traditional pizza, characterized by its use of barbecue sauce as the base instead of the usual tomato sauce. This gives the pizza a distinctive sweet, smoky, and tangy profile

Pepperoni pizza

Pepperoni pizza is a universally recognized and immensely popular type of pizza, particularly famous in North America. It's a classic for good reason, offering a harmonious blend of savory, slightly spicy, and deeply comforting flavors.

Hawaiian pizza

Hawaiian pizza is a distinctive and often debated pizza variety that combines the sweet and savory. It's known for its unique set of toppings
